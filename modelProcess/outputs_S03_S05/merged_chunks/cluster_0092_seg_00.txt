This article represents the views of CSO, a news media platform under IDG Consulting. Security threats are constantly evolving and tend to follow trends. As long as a certain type of threat is highly successful and profitable, cybercriminals will imitate it. Therefore, the best defense is to identify these threat trends and take the initiative in threat detection. In line with this perspective, Gartner rated the "Top 11 Information Security Technologies for 2017" in June. We focused on the hottest technology categories mentioned by Gartner for 2017, delved into the latest cybersecurity products and services, and selected 22 of the most innovative and practical products for 2017. These products have all been tested in local test platforms or production environments provided by the vendors. They address some of the most destructive security threats currently. Category: Deception Technology
Deception technology is an emerging field, and simple and effective deployment technologies have often been at a bottleneck. Acalvio ShadowPlex sets an example in overcoming these technical difficulties. It provides customers with an unlimited number of deception assets, avoiding frequent maintenance and cost inputs. These deception points can be activated immediately when needed, locking onto attackers and continuously tracking them to obtain valuable threat intelligence.
