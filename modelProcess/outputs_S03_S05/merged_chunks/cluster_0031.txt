IBM's 2023 Data Breach Cost Report indicates that organizations using AI and automated security tools can reduce data breach costs by an average of $1.76 million. Additionally, mature DevSecOps practices can lower average data breach costs by $1.68 million compared to less mature organizations. The report also highlights that the data breach lifecycle is shortened by 108 days on average for organizations utilizing these tools. Furthermore, the cost of data breaches has been increasing, with the global average reaching $4.45 million in 2023, up 15% from 2020. The report also notes that breaches involving law enforcement can save an average of $470,000 in costs.
