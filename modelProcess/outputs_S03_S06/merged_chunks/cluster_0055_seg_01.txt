To strengthen consumer privacy rights and data security, the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) have been enacted and passed. Many companies, lacking awareness and capability for user privacy and data protection, urgently need to respond to various compliance requirements or face severe penalties. The data privacy security company BigID won the RSAC 2018 Innovation Sandbox, and this year’s Innovation Sandbox also focuses on innovation in the field of data privacy, indicating a strong demand for innovation in data privacy protection in the security sector. Privacy protection and data security have become important components of corporate security capability building. However, enhancing data privacy protection in a complex IT system environment poses a significant challenge to traditional data loss prevention technologies and products, adding a heavy burden to corporate data management. Large enterprises, with their complex internal network environments and data storage architectures, require technical support across multiple stages such as data discovery, management, and classification for effective data privacy protection.
