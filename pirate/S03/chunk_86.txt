From June 6-9, 2022, the RSA Conference 2022 will be held in San Francisco, USA. As an annual feast for the global cybersecurity industry, the RSAC conference is hailed as the "Olympics" of the security world and serves as one of the key indicators of cybersecurity trends. Since its inception in 1991, the RSAC conference has grown from a small cryptography forum to become the top global information security conference today, entering its 31st year. It attracts cybersecurity companies, experts, geeks, and outstanding entrepreneurs from around the world.
Among the various events, the most eye-catching is the RSAC Innovation Sandbox Contest, which represents the direction of technological innovation in the cybersecurity industry and is a key focus of capital investment, drawing significant attention from the global cybersecurity community. Previously, the RSAC official had announced the list of the top 10 finalists for the Innovation Sandbox, including Araali Networks, BastionZero, Cado Security, Cycode, Dasera, Lightspin, Neosec, Sevco Security, Talon Cyber Security, and Torq. These finalists cover multiple areas such as cloud-native security, access security, software supply chain security, data governance, API security, and security operations.
